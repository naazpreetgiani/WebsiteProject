About Basketball

History

In December 1891, James Naismith, a Canadian professor of physical education was trying to keep his gym class active on a rainy day. He created a sport (basketball) in which players would pass a ball to teammates.

Dribbling was not part of the original game except for the "bounce pass" due to the asymmetric shape of the early balls.

Rules and regulations

There are many rules in basketball. When these rules are violated, the team in which who makes the violation forfeits possession of the ball. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Taking more than two steps without dribbling (travel)
  • Dribbling with 2 hands or dribbling twice with a stop in between (double dribble)
  • Placing a hand on the bottom of a ball within a dribble (carry)
